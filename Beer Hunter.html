Turret Defender

Game Design 3 Final Vertical Slice

Overview

Turret Defender is a mostly finished vertical slice of an arcade game that was created as a concept for the Game Design 3 course at DAE. You play as a turret defending against waves of enemies, managing resources and maintaining your defenses. Can you get the high score?

My Role

This project was a solo effort, where I handled all aspects of the game design and implementation. I designed the game mechanics, created the art assets, and implemented the game logic using Unity. The project was a great opportunity to showcase my skills in game design and development, and I learned a lot about balancing gameplay and creating engaging mechanics.
