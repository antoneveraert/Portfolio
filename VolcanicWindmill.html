Volcanic Windmill

Game Art 3 Project

Overview

This volcanic windmill is also a mandatory part of the Game Art 3 course. The concept was a fluffy woodland environment within a more volcanic setting. This is obviously not possible, but I decided to give it a more whimsical twist by incorporating bright colours and stylized lava.

My Role

This is a solo project which was mandatory for my Game Art 3 course. I was responsible for all aspects of the project, including concept development, asset creation, and implementation.
