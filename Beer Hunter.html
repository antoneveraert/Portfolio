Beer Hunter

VR Western Arcade Shooter

Overview

Beer Hunter is a VR arcade shooter game I made for an assignment in my Game Production 2 course. The assignment was to create a marketing campaign. I thought having the game would make things a lot easier. So, using online assets, I built a vertical slice of the game. This was my first time working with VR, I learned a lot and in about 12 hours this is what i managed to make.

My Role

The assignment was in teams but this project i made by myself. I based everything of a game design document we received from the teacher. it was a great opportunity to learn about VR development and showcase my skills in prototyping and programming.
