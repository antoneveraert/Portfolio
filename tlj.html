Two Left Jams

2025 Creaweek Game Jam Winner

Overview

Two Left Jams is an award-winning game jam project created for the 2025 DAE Creaweek game jam. The theme prompt was "Spread it", we took that quite literally. The game features innovative gameplay mechanics and a unique art style, showcasing the talents of the development team. The time limit on this project was 2 days, and we were able to create a fully functional game with a unique concept and engaging gameplay. The game was well-received by the judges and players alike, earning us 1st place for the judges and 2nd place for the community.

My Role

In this Game Jam Project, I took on the role of UX programmer and trouble-shooter. I was responsible for implementing anything the user would directly interact with such as the hand and camera controls, Interacting with items and fixing bugs on my own work and that of others.
