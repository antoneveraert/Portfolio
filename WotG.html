Warden Of The Garden

2024 Game Design Game Jam Winner

Overview

Warden Of The Garden is an arcade defense game that won the 2024 Game Design Game Jam. Players take on the role of the royal gardener who must protect their royal garden from waves of invasive pests. The game features unique tools and enemies that are both realistic in their use and scaling in design. The game was developed in a short time frame (less than 48 hours), showcasing the team's ability to create a polished and engaging experience under pressure. The game received positive feedback for its innovative mechanics and engaging gameplay, earning us the top spot in the competition. It also features a Unique toon shading art style that enhances the visual appeal while saving us a lot of time. We worked hard and had a lot of fun creating this game, we never took it too seriously, but we were able to create a polished and engaging experience under pressure.

My Role

In this team of 3, I served as game designer, gameplay programmer and shader programmer. I was responsible for developing the core gameplay mechanics, implementing the enemies, creating new ideas and implementing art and shader effects.
