Game Art 2 Exam Project

Overview

Fluffy Nature is a serene and visually captivating game environment created as a part of my Game Art 3 exam project. The project focuses on creating good shaders, lighting and overall good environmental assets. The environment I envisioned is a lush, green forest filled with tall grass and a vibrant waterfall. A peaceful campsite is nestled within the trees, complete with a cozy tent and a warm campfire. The scene is designed to evoke a sense of tranquility and connection with nature. Art isn't where my focus lies, but I do have a solid understanding and can do an adequate job when needed.

My Role

This is a solo project which was mandatory for my Game Art 3 course. I was responsible for all aspects of the project, including concept development, asset creation, and implementation.
